apartment in an office

If the general director is the founder, then registration of the legal address of the legal entity at the place of residence of the general director is provided. To do this, you need to obtain written consent from all co-owners of the apartment. There are no risks in this. Most small entrepreneurs register their organizations in this way.

Entity

A legal entity has the right to conduct its activities and be registered in a residential apartment only if the general director is registered at this address. In other cases, placing an office in a residential apartment is fraught with fines and problems. A legal entity can rent an apartment, but it can only be used as a living space: for example, sublet tenants or provide this apartment for employees to live.

About

It is impossible to register a legal entity in a residential apartment; only individual entrepreneurial activity or professional activity is allowed. Obstacles to placing an office in a residential area can be: objections of other persons, both having the right to use the apartment, and who are residents of an apartment building; violation of sanitary and hygienic rules: for example, when placing industrial production; carrying out missionary activities.

Economic activity

Economic activity requires that organizations and individual entrepreneurs have a permanent address of their registration, the so-called anchor point. What is it for? This is important for the tax office for sending official correspondence and for conducting field checks.
